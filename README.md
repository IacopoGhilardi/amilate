# Amilate

> Never be late again.

Amilate is your personal departure assistant. It monitors your calendar, tracks real-time traffic and road conditions, and tells you exactly when to leave — so you always arrive on time, every time.

---

## How it works

Connect your calendar. Set your preferences — how early you like to arrive, your usual transport mode, anything else that matters to you. Amilate takes care of the rest.

When it's time to go, you'll know.

- **Smart departure alerts** — based on live traffic, not just estimated travel time
- **Personalized to you** — early arrival buffer, transport preferences, and more
- **Always up to date** — conditions change, and so do your alerts
